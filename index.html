Introduction

The growing number of personal vehicles in urban areas near workplaces has led to parking challenges, causing gridlock and congestion. An intelligent parking management system, guided by IoT principles, aims to alleviate these issues. This system incorporates machine learning, cloud computing, networking, embedded systems, and web development. It features a web portal for real-time parking updates, CCTV for vehicle security, and parking utilization analysis. Additionally, it includes a license plate recognition device to reduce search time, fuel consumption, traffic, and pollution. Improved parking availability enhances productivity and positively impacts the economy.

Administrator Login

The portal allows administrators to log in and view real-time information about the parking slots, such as occupancy, availability, and location.

Slot Manager Function

This feature enables administrators to assign, modify, or delete parking slots for different companies and vehicle types.

Parking History

The portal displays the parking history of each vehicle, including the arrival and departure times, the parking duration, and the parking fee.

Vehicle Details

It shows the details of each vehicle, such as the registration number, the owner name, the company name, and the vehicle type.

Employee Details

The portal provides details of each employee, such as the name, the company name, the contact number, and the vehicle registration number.

Live Slots Page

This page shows the current status of each parking slot, using different colors to indicate whether the slot is occupied, vacant or reserved

Alert System

The portal has an alert system that notifies the administrators of any suspicious or abnormal activities, such as parking violations, theft attempts, or harmful sounds.
